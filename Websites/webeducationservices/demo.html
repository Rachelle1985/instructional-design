AI Activity

AI Workplace Coach

Practice real workplace situations using the prompt below with ChatGPT or your organization's AI assistant.

Your Prompt
Act as a workplace coach. Present me with three realistic workplace scenarios involving communication, teamwork, inclusion, or conflict resolution. After I respond to each scenario: 1. Evaluate my response. 2. Explain what I did well. 3. Suggest ways I could improve. 4. Connect your feedback to respectful workplace practices. Present one scenario at a time and wait for my response before continuing.
Reflection Questions

After completing the exercise, reflect on:

  • 1 What did you learn from this activity?
  • 2 Which scenario was most challenging, and why?
  • 3 How will you apply these skills in your day-to-day work?
