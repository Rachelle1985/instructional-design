Sales Enablement Deck Series

A 12-part microlearning series in Google Slides — shareable, trackable, and easy for managers to update the moment the product changes.

Google Slides Distributed sales team Instructional design & templates 5 weeks
The Challenge

Fast-moving product, slow-moving training

A B2B sales organization with about 40 field and inside reps needed constant enablement, but the product changed faster than any traditional course could keep up. Full authoring tools were overkill and locked content behind a specialist — every update meant waiting on someone else.

Managers needed to own the material: update a price, swap a competitor slide, add a new objection-handling tip, and reshare in minutes — without learning new software.

Project Snapshot

  • ClientB2B sales organization · ~40 reps
  • AudienceField & inside sales reps, multi-region
  • Deliverable12 branded microlearning decks
  • Format5–7 min per module
The Approach

The right-sized tool, done right

Google Slides was a deliberate choice: zero cost, instantly shareable, and a tool the whole team already knew. The win wasn't the platform — it was the instructional design on top of it. I built a branded master template and a 12-part series of focused, single-objective modules: positioning, discovery, objection handling, competitive plays, and more.

Built to be owned and updated

Each deck used locked layout elements and clearly editable content zones, so managers could update facts without breaking the design. Trackable share links and embedded job aids meant reps could pull up exactly the right module mid-deal — and leadership could see what was being used.
