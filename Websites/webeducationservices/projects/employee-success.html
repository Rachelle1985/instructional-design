The Challenge

A handbook nobody opened

A ~30-person local service business onboarded every new hire with the same artifact: a 40-page PDF handbook sitting on a shared drive. With a couple of new hires every month, it checked a box but taught almost nothing — there was no way to know if anyone read it, understood it, or could apply it.

They wanted onboarding that was engaging enough to finish, rigorous enough to measure, and affordable enough to run without an enterprise LMS contract.

Project Snapshot

  • ClientLocal service business · ~30 employees
  • AudienceEvery new hire (~2–3/month)
  • DeliverableSelf-hosted interactive web app
  • ExtrasLive analytics + certificate
The Approach

From static document to living experience

I rebuilt the handbook as a custom, browser-based onboarding program — no LMS subscription required. The content became an interactive module with scenario-based decisions, knowledge checks with instant feedback, and a hands-on AI coaching activity that shows employees how to use AI tools responsibly from day one.

Measurement built in

The real differentiator is the analytics layer. As a learner moves through the experience, the program quietly tracks time per section, quiz attempts, and where people struggle — then surfaces plain-English flags so a manager knows exactly what follow-up conversation to have. It finishes with a printable, branded certificate for HR records.
