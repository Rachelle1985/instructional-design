Compliance Training — Healthcare Onboarding

A branching, scenario-based course that turned a 60-page policy manual into decisions clinical staff actually remember — and complete.

Articulate Storyline 360 Healthcare End-to-end design & development 6 weeks
The Challenge

A policy manual nobody finished

A regional health system — roughly 1,200 employees across six clinic sites — onboarded every new clinical and administrative hire with the same 60-page compliance manual and a paper sign-off sheet. With about 25 new hires a month, completion was low, retention was lower, and the organization was carrying real HIPAA and OSHA exposure: staff were attesting to policies they hadn't truly absorbed.

The goal wasn't to add more content. It was to make the existing policies stick: help new hires recognize the right action in the messy, real-world moments where compliance actually happens.

Project Snapshot

  • ClientRegional health system · ~1,200 staff, 6 sites
  • AudienceNew clinical & admin hires (~25/month)
  • ScopeHIPAA, OSHA, code of conduct, safety
  • Seat time~28 minutes · SCORM 1.2/2004
The Approach

Scenario-first, decision-driven design

Instead of presenting policy and quizzing on it afterward, every objective was framed as a workplace scenario. Learners step into realistic situations — a coworker oversharing patient information, an unattended workstation, a reporting dilemma — and choose how to respond, then see the consequences of that choice play out.

Built for accountability

The course was packaged with full SCORM tracking so HR could verify genuine completion and assessment scores, not just a checkbox. Custom-illustrated characters and consistent branding made the experience feel like the organization's own, and the entire course was authored to WCAG 2.1 AA so every new hire could access it.

Key Features

What was built

Branching scenarios

Decision points with realistic consequences using Storyline variables and triggers — no two wrong answers feel the same.

Custom characters

Illustrated staff and patients that reflect a real clinical setting, making situations relatable rather than abstract.

SCORM tracking & assessment

Scored knowledge checks and completion data pushed to the LMS for clean compliance records.
